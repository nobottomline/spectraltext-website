Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a voice message?

Open the "Recording" tab in the app, press the large microphone button and start speaking. When you're done, press the "Stop" button. The app will automatically recognize speech and display the text.

What languages does speech recognition support?

The app supports many languages, including English, Russian, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Chinese, and many others. Select the desired language in settings before starting the recording.

Where are my recordings and transcriptions stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not send your recordings and transcriptions to servers. Audio recordings are saved temporarily for processing, and transcriptions are saved in the app's history.

How do I open a file in the app?

Go to the "My Files" tab and press the "Open File" button. You can select a file from other apps or use the built-in file manager. The app supports TXT, MD, PDF formats and other text formats.

How do I use text-to-speech?

Open a file or transcription, press the "Speak" button and select parameters (speed, voice). The app will start reading the text. You can pause, rewind, or stop playback at any time.

Do I need internet to use the app?

Internet is not required for core functions (reading files, text-to-speech, viewing history). However, internet may be required for speech recognition, as it uses Apple (iOS) and Google (Android) speech recognition services.

How do I delete a saved transcription?

Open the "History" tab, find the desired transcription and swipe left (on iOS) or press it and select "Delete" (on Android). You can also delete all transcriptions at once in settings.

How do I share a transcription or file?

Press on the transcription or file, then select the "Share" button. You can send the text as a text file or share it through any available app on your device.

The app doesn't recognize speech correctly. What should I do?

Make sure you speak clearly and in a quiet environment. Check that the correct recognition language is selected. Also make sure that microphone permission has been granted to the app.

How do I update the app?

The app updates automatically through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). You can also check for updates manually in the settings of the respective app store.

User Guide

Getting Started

  1. Download and install the Spectral Text app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. On first launch, allow the app access to the microphone (for voice recording feature)
  3. Take a brief tour of the app to learn about its features
  4. Start using: record voice, open files, listen to text

App Permissions

The app may require the following permissions for full functionality:

  • Microphone: required for recording voice messages and transcribing them to text
  • Speech Recognition: required for converting voice to text
  • File Access: required for opening and saving files

Important: All permissions are requested only when necessary and only after your explicit action. You can revoke permissions at any time through the operating system settings.

Tips for Best Quality

  • Speak clearly and distinctly, avoid background noise
  • Keep the device 20-30 cm away from your mouth
  • Use headphones with a microphone for recording in noisy places
  • Make sure the correct recognition language is selected
  • Check your internet connection before using speech recognition

Note: Speech recognition quality depends on many factors: pronunciation clarity, background noise presence, microphone quality, and internet speed (if online recognition is used).
